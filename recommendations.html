Art Advocacy & Organizations

National Coalition Against Censorship

Advocacy National

The National Coalition Against Censorship (NCAC) is a vital organization that defends freedom of thought, inquiry, and expression. They work tirelessly to protect artists' rights and combat censorship in all its forms.

Why I recommend them: Their advocacy work ensures that artists can create without fear of censorship, making them essential for maintaining artistic freedom in our society.

Visit NCAC →

Don't Delete Art

Preservation Digital

Don't Delete Art is a movement dedicated to preserving and protecting artistic expression in the digital age. They work to prevent the removal and censorship of art across digital platforms.

Why I recommend them: In our increasingly digital world, their work to preserve artistic expression online is crucial for future generations of artists and art lovers.

Rhizome

Local Queer Community

Rhizome is a vibrant DC-based queer art gallery that creates space for LGBTQ+ artists and allies to showcase their work and build community. This gallery champions diverse queer voices and provides a vital platform for artistic expression that might not find space in mainstream venues.

Location: Washington, DC

Highlights: Queer artist showcases, community events, inclusive exhibition programming, supporting emerging LGBTQ+ artists
