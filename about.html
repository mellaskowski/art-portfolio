Hello, I'm Laz

Welcome to my creative universe, playhouse, puppetry workshop, and digital art studio! I'm a passionate visual artist and designer with a love for bringing ideas to life through digital and physical mediums. My work spans across various disciplines, from animated pixel art and album cover designs to social impact illustrations and art around disability.

My artistic journey began with a fascination for the creative communication one can achieve through art. I believe that art has the power to inspire, and create meaningful connections globally by its inherent nature of understanding. Whether I'm designing an album cover that captures the essence of music, creating animations that mesmerize viewers, or developing illustrations for important social causes, every piece tells a story.

What sets my work apart is the attention to detail and the emotional depth I bring to each project. I'm particularly drawn to projects that combine aesthetic beauty with purposeful messaging, such as healthcare awareness campaigns and environmental justice impact designs. My goal is to create art that not only looks stunning but also makes a difference.

"Remember that consciousness is power. Consciousness is education and knowledge. Consciousness is becoming aware. It is the perfect vehicle for students to understand their role in the struggle for justice." - Yuri Kochiyama

When I'm not creating digital art, you can find me exploring queer dharama, meditation, building a new software project, or petting my cat. I'm propelled forward by one of my role models, Yuri Kochiyama, who reminds me that consciousness is power and that we all have a role to play in the fight for justice. I'm always excited to take on new creative journeys and push the boundaries of what's reality today versus the future of tomorrow.
